Data Deletion Instructions

How to request deletion of your personal data

At Nexofy Digital, we respect your right to privacy and data control. This page provides instructions on how to request the deletion of your personal data from our systems.

What Data Can Be Deleted?

You can request the deletion of the following types of data:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number
  • Communication Records: Messages sent through contact forms
  • Account Data: If you have an account with us
  • Project Information: Non-essential project communications
  • Marketing Data: Newsletter subscriptions and preferences

⚠️ Important Notice

Data We Must Retain:

  • Financial records (invoices, payment records) - Required by law for 7 years
  • Contractual agreements - Required for legal compliance
  • Dispute resolution records - Required for ongoing or potential legal matters
  • Essential business records - Required for tax and audit purposes

How to Request Data Deletion

Follow these simple steps to request deletion of your data:

1Send an Email Request

Email us at: projects@nexofydigital.com

2Include Required Information

Your email must include:

  • Subject Line: "Data Deletion Request"
  • Your full name
  • Email address associated with your data
  • Phone number (if applicable)
  • Specific data you want deleted (or request "all personal data")
  • Confirmation that you are the data owner

3Identity Verification

For security purposes, we may need to verify your identity before processing your request. This may involve:

  • Confirming details from previous interactions
  • Requesting additional identification
  • Verifying ownership of the email address

4Processing Time

We will process your request within:

  • Standard Request: 30 days
  • Complex Request: Up to 60 days (we will notify you if extension is needed)

5Confirmation

You will receive a confirmation email once:

  • We receive your request (within 2 business days)
  • Your identity is verified
  • Your data has been deleted

Email Template

You can use this template for your data deletion request:

Subject: Data Deletion Request Dear Nexofy Digital Team, I am writing to request the deletion of my personal data from your systems. My Details: - Full Name: [Your Name] - Email Address: [Your Email] - Phone Number: [Your Phone] (if provided) I request deletion of: [Select one] ☐ All my personal data ☐ Specific data: [List specific data] I confirm that I am the owner of this data and authorize its deletion. Thank you for processing this request. Best regards, [Your Name]

What Happens After Deletion?

Once your data is deleted:

  • Your personal information will be permanently removed from our active systems
  • Backup systems will be purged within 90 days
  • You will no longer receive marketing communications (if subscribed)
  • Your account (if any) will be deactivated
  • You may need to re-provide information for future services

Data Retention Exceptions

We may retain certain data if:

  • Required by law (financial records, tax documents)
  • Necessary for legal claims or disputes
  • Part of an ongoing contract or project
  • Essential for security and fraud prevention
  • Anonymized for statistical purposes (non-identifiable)

Third-Party Services

If you used our services that integrate with third-party platforms (e.g., Facebook, WhatsApp Business), you may also need to:

  • Request deletion directly from those platforms
  • Revoke app permissions in your account settings
  • Contact their support for data removal

We will assist you with identifying relevant third parties upon request.

Your Rights

Under data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data (right to be forgotten)
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data
  • Objection: Object to certain processing activities
  • Restriction: Request limitation on data processing
